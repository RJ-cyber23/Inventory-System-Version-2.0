# 📦 Inventory System V2.0 🚀
> **The Enterprise-Grade Architecture for Modern Logistics.**

---

## 🌟 Why Version 2.0?
This isn't just a database; it's a **Well Structured**. Designed to handle complex business logic from the moment a product is ordered from a supplier to the second it's sold to a customer.

### 🔥 Key Features
- **🎯 Precision Tracking:** Product vs. Variant logic (size, color, brand).
- **🛡️ Audit-Ready:** Integrated Physical Inventory Audit system to kill "Ghost Stock."
- **📊 Business Intelligence:** Structured for future automated Invoicing and Sales reporting.
- **⚡ High Performance:** Optimized for **Every Relational Database** with 3rd Normal Form (3NF) standards.
